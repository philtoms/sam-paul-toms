Midnight Sessions was born from a series of late-night recording sessions during the winter of 2025. Each track began as an improvisation, later refined and layered with synthesizers, field recordings, and found sounds from the city.

The album explores themes of solitude, urban exploration, and the liminal space between night and day. It's best listened to in full, from dusk to dawn.
